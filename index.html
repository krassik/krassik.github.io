Multichannel Decoding for Mobile Devices

In the days of the 32-bit ARM, this project was aiming to display a handful of video streams simultaneously on a mobile device screen similar to how CCTV stations are used to monitor arrays of security cameras. Major constraint for this outcome had been the capability of the microprocessor for which the mobile operating systems restricted to one at a time, hence for decoding of single stream only. On this video I experiment with the encoding schemes and container formats for mobile devices. In the aftermath, solution circumventing the ARM limitation was discovered and became the cornerstone for a successful mobile product.
